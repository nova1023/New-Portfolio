Battle Bay

Battle Bay is a game in which players buy items at low prices then sell them for a profit. Players will have to create an account in order to start playing. Scripted A.I. will bid once items are posted up for sale by players. Individual statistics are available in the user's H.Q. and a leaderboard will keep track who has earned the most profit.

Mostly Cryptic

Mostly Cryptic is an online multiplayer card game. Players give out a vague clue pertaining to a card in their hand. Other players will select a card from their hand to try and fool other players from choosing the card from the clue-giver.

Diablo 2 Trivia

A timed trivia game testing the player's knowledge of the ARPG, Diablo 2. This application uses background music.
